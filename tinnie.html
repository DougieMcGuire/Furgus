Our Story

More Than Daycare — It's a Community

Tinnie's Daycare is a family-centered home daycare in Fargo, North Dakota dedicated to empowering children through cultural enrichment, hands-on learning, and loving care.

  • ✓Culturally enriched curriculum — celebrating diversity through stories, music, and art
  • ✓Play-based learning — developing curiosity, creativity, and social skills
  • ✓Small group sizes — every child receives individual attention and care
  • ✓Flexible scheduling — drop-in and weekend options to fit your family
  • ✓Safe, nurturing environment — a true home-away-from-home

"Empowering Children Through Culture and Play"

We go beyond basic childcare. At Tinnie's, children are encouraged to explore their identities, develop a love of learning, and build friendships that last a lifetime.

What We Offer

Programs Built for Every Stage

From newborns to preschoolers, our age-appropriate programs nurture growth at every developmental milestone.

👶

Infant Care

Ages 0–12 months

Gentle, responsive care designed to support your newborn's rapid development in a safe, loving home setting.

  • Sensory exploration activities
  • Consistent routines for healthy development
  • Tummy time & motor skill support
  • Regular parent updates
🧸

Toddler Program

Ages 1–3 years

Active, curious toddlers thrive with structured play, language-rich activities, and gentle social learning.

  • Language & communication building
  • Cultural music, art & stories
  • Social-emotional skill development
  • Outdoor & active play time
🎒

Preschool Prep

Ages 3–5 years

School-readiness skills through play — letters, numbers, science exploration, and cultural discovery every day.

  • Early literacy & numeracy
  • Multicultural learning projects
  • Fine & gross motor activities
  • Kindergarten transition support
